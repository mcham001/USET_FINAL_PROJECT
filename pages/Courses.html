Our Popular Courses

Engineering Department

This department by design offers different course levels in the field of engineering from Gambia Skills Qualification (GSQ) to Certificate, Diploma and Technician Diploma with a focus to develop student expertise in electronics and other related disciplines like communication, computer system and electrical power engineering. Students are prepared for employment or further education especially in our new courses like BSc-Electrical Electronic Engineering, BSc-Mechanical Engineering, HND Civil Engineering/Electronics, HND Mechanical Engineering and HND Electrical/Electronic Engineering.
