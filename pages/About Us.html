About Us

The Gambia University of Applied Science, Engineering, and Technology (USET) is a leading institution in The Gambia, dedicated to fostering innovation, technical excellence, and entrepreneurship. Established in 2020 as an evolution of the Gambia Technical Training Institute (GTTI), USET builds on a rich legacy of over four decades in technical and vocational education.

At Uset, we drive progress through scientific discovery, engineering, technology, and entrepreneurial innovation.

Who We Are

Founded on a vision of excellence, The USET community is driven by a share purpose. To make a better Gambia and the region through scientific discovery and engineering advancement (COSE), cutting-edge technical entrepreneurship and innovation for economic growth (IOIE).
